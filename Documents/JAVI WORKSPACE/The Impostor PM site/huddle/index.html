The Format

09:30

Check-in & Context Setting

Coffee, introductions, and establishing psychological safety rules. We define the "Problem Statements" for the day.

10:00

Hot Seats (Round 1)

Individual deep dives. One person presents a challenge, the group dissects it with Socratic questioning.

11:15

Bio Break & Networking

Casual conversation to decompress and connect one-on-one.

11:30

Hot Seats (Round 2) & Synthesis

Second round of challenges followed by a guided synthesis of key takeaways and action items.

12:30

Close

Leave with clarity.

Huddle is for you if:

1

You are a Senior PM or above

You know the basics. You aren't looking for "How to write a user story." You're dealing with organizational complexity.

2

You feel stuck or isolated

You're the only PM in your startup, or you feel like you can't be vulnerable with your current team.

3

You are willing to share

This is participatory. You must be willing to bring a real problem to the table and give feedback to others.

4

You value distinct perspectives

You understand that the answer isn't always "it depends," but finding the context that matters.
